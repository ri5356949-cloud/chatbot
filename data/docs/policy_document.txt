
COMPANY LEAVE POLICY
Version 1.0 | January 2025

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Maximum consecutive leave allowed is 15 days unless approved by department head.

2. SICK LEAVE
Employees can take up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days. Sick leave cannot be carried forward to next year.

3. CASUAL LEAVE
5 days of casual leave are provided per year for personal matters. These can be taken without prior approval but must be informed to the immediate supervisor. Casual leave can be taken in half-day increments.

4. MATERNITY/PATERNITY LEAVE
Female employees: 16 weeks paid maternity leave
Male employees: 2 weeks paid paternity leave
Must be availed within 6 months of childbirth.

5. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
3 days paid leave in case of immediate family member's death. Extended unpaid leave can be requested through HR.

6. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
Company observes 15 national holidays per year. List is published at the start of each calendar year.

For questions, contact HR at hr@company.com
